FIELD GUIDE

Goal
Trade shots until one dragon falls. Campaign: climb stages, earn EXP and gold, level up, buy gear. 2-Player Duel: two dragons, one device — pass and play.
Your turn
Move with ◀ ▶ (or A/D) — drains stamina, refilled next turn.
Aim with ＋/− (or W/S). Fire by holding FIRE (or Space) to charge power, release to launch — or drag back from your dragon like a slingshot.
Firing, healing, shielding, or blinking ends your turn. Potions don't.
Your previous shot's angle, power and wind stay on screen, and a gold ✕ marks where it landed — read the new wind and correct from your last shot, artillery style. A gold line on FIRE marks your last power.
Wind, terrain & obstacles
The pennant shows the wind — it bends every shot and changes each turn. The whole battlefield is on screen by default; tap 🎯 (or press Z) for a close-up action camera that follows movement and shots. Blasts carve craters; dragons take fall damage. Stone spires block your path and floating rocks block your shots — arc over them or blast them apart. Some battles rage over meadow, cinder wastes, or frozen reaches.
Skills & MP
Skills cost MP (+14 regained per turn). Each dragon has two signature skills — the second unlocks at level 4: life-stealing boomerangs, freezing slabs, splitting meteors, sky bolts, bouncing stones, poison clouds, walls of ice, even a shadow-step escape.
Gear & stats
The shop sells permanent gear: ATK raises damage · DEF lowers damage taken · AGI extends stamina · LUK raises crit chance.
